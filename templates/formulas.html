Formula Reference

Differentiation Formulas

  • \( \frac{d}{dx} x^n = nx^{n-1} \)
  • \( \frac{d}{dx} \sin x = \cos x \)
  • \( \frac{d}{dx} \cos x = -\sin x \)
  • \( \frac{d}{dx} e^x = e^x \)
  • \( \frac{d}{dx} \ln x = \frac{1}{x} \)
  • \( \frac{d}{dx} \tan x = \sec^2 x \)
  • \( \text{Chain Rule: } \frac{d}{dx} f(g(x)) = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x) \)
  • \( \text{Product Rule: } \frac{d}{dx}[uv] = u'v + uv' \)
  • \( \text{Quotient Rule: } \frac{d}{dx}\left[\frac{u}{v}\right] = \frac{u'v - uv'}{v^2} \)

Integration Formulas

  • \( \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \)
  • \( \int \sin x \, dx = -\cos x + C \)
  • \( \int \cos x \, dx = \sin x + C \)
  • \( \int e^x \, dx = e^x + C \)
  • \( \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln|x| + C \)
  • \( \int \sec^2 x \, dx = \tan x + C \)
  • \( \int a^x \, dx = \frac{a^x}{\ln a} + C \)

Limits Formulas

  • \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1 \)
  • \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x}{x} = 0 \)
  • \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{x}\right)^x = e \)
  • \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 1}{x} = 1 \)
  • \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1+x)}{x} = 1 \)
  • \( \text{L'Hôpital's Rule: } \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x \to a} \frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)} \)

Series & Sequences

  • \( \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x^n = \frac{1}{1-x}, \quad |x| < 1 \)
  • \( e^x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!} \)
  • \( \sin x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)!} \)
  • \( \cos x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n x^{2n}}{(2n)!} \)
  • \( \ln(1+x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n+1} x^n}{n}, \quad |x| \leq 1 \)
  • \( \text{Ratio Test: Series converges if } \lim_{n \to \infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| < 1 \)
